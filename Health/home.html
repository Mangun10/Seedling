Integrating health education into the school curriculum is crucial for promoting overall well-being among students. It equips them with essential knowledge and skills to make informed decisions about their health. Incorporating topics such as nutrition, physical activity, mental health, sexual education, and substance abuse prevention helps students develop lifelong healthy habits. Additionally, integrating health education fosters a positive school environment and contributes to academic success by addressing factors that may hinder learning, such as poor nutrition or mental health issues.
